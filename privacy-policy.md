# AxeSentry Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 17, 2026**

AxeSentry ("the app") is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

## The short version
AxeSentry does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Everything the app does happens on your own device and your own local network.

## What the app accesses
- **Your local network.** AxeSentry communicates directly with your solo Bitcoin miners over your home Wi-Fi using the AxeOS API. This is required to read miner status and send control commands (such as reboot or fan settings). iOS asks for your permission the first time the app scans your network.
- **On-device storage.** Your list of miners and your alert preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage.
- **iCloud (optional).** If you are signed into iCloud, your miner list may sync across your own Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy and is accessible only to you. AxeSentry's developer cannot see it.
- **Notifications.** If you enable notifications, alerts are generated and delivered locally by your device. No alert content is sent to any server operated by the developer.

## What the app does NOT do
- It does not collect personal information.
- It does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
- It does not show advertising.
- It does not transmit your data to the developer or any third party.
- It does not require an account or sign-in.

## Data sharing
None. Because no data is collected, none is shared or sold.

## Children's privacy
AxeSentry does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Third-party services
AxeSentry can optionally query a public blockchain data source to display current network difficulty for the block-odds estimate. These requests contain no personal information.
