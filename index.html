Frequently asked

  • My miners aren't showing up. Make sure your iPhone or iPad is on the same Wi-Fi network as your miners, and allow local-network access when iOS prompts. Then use Scan, or add a miner by its IP address.
  • Which devices are supported? Bitaxe and NerdAxe / NerdQAxe miners running the AxeOS API.
  • Do alerts work when the app is closed? Yes, on a best-effort basis. iOS controls how often background checks run, so background alerts may be delayed. Keep Notifications and Background App Refresh enabled for AxeSentry in iOS Settings.
  • Does AxeSentry collect my data? No. It works entirely on your local network with no account and no tracking. See the Privacy Policy.
